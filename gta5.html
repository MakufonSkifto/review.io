Grand Theft Auto V is a continue game for the Grand Theft Auto Series. In this game, Rockstar again proves that they can make good open world games. The game has an gigantic open-world map. There are 3 characters: Michael, Trevor, Franlin. Michael and Trevor used to work together as a heist team. Michael "dies" in an heist. I wrote dies in quotation mark because he actually makes a fake funeral just to get out of sight and have a good life in Los Santos, the main city of the game.
